G-Smart Internet Safety
 

G-Smart devices help parents to control the activities of their children when accessing the Internet.
- Dad, Mom self-regulate the Internet access time of children.
- Dad, Mom easily customize whether or not to allow children access to the site you do not want children to use.
- Dad, Mom can see the Internet access activities when going outside.
- Dad, Mom can see the history of accessing websites from the computer and the phone they have visited.
- Allows setting time for children to access Internet during the day.
- Limited access to about 02 million web pages with inappropriate content for children.
- Incorporates the Secure Content Control feature of videos, Google, Bing
- Can control such as turn off Internet access, view remote access history, ...
- Can create multiple Internet access groups suitable for different ages
- Managing the activities of devices such as computers, smart phones, tablets, Smart TV.
- G-Smart is available with all network operators.
- G-Smart supports connections via Wifi and network cables.
